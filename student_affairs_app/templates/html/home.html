Student Affairs

The Student Affairs office at Cairo University is responsible for managing the non-academic aspects of student life.
The office promotes student involvement in extracurricular activities, including student organizations, clubs, and societies. The office also organizes cultural and sports events to showcase student talents and creativity.

Additionally, the office provides counseling and psychological services to support student well-being and manages housing and residential services.
Overall, the Student Affairs office at Cairo University plays a critical role in enhancing the student experience by offering a wide range of services and resources to support student success and engagement.
